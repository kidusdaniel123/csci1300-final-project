# Extra Credit Demonstration Guide

This version attempts the maximum 11 points of pre-made extra credit.

## Option 4 — Weather System with Gameplay Effects (+5)

Weather is stored in `Game::currentWeather` and generated by `Game::generateWeather()` at the beginning of each day.

Weather effects:

- **Sunny:** farming costs less energy and can produce a bonus Golden Parsnip.
- **Rainy:** fishing costs less energy, fishing success increases, and foraging may produce a bonus Rain Mushroom.
- **Stormy:** fishing is harder and the mine entrance is closed for mining.

The current weather appears on the main dashboard.

## Option 6 — Smarter Bundle Planner (+6)

Choose **Smart Bundle Planner** from the main menu. The planner:

- Checks which bundle categories are still missing.
- Checks whether the player already owns a needed category.
- Recommends the appropriate location, villager, or action.
- Changes its advice based on the current weather.
- Warns the player that Joja shortcuts affect the ending.

The main function is `Game::displayBundlePlanner()`.

## Quick Interview Demo

1. Start the game and point out the weather on the dashboard.
2. Select **Smart Bundle Planner** from the menu.
3. Show that it recommends actions for all missing categories.
4. End the day to generate new weather.
5. Visit a location affected by that weather, such as the Farm, Beach, Forest, or Mines.
